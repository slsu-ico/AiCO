 I.  Mandate   The  Information  and  Communications  Office  (ICO)  of  Southern  Luzon  State  University  (SLSU)  is  
established
 
under
 
BOR
 
Resolution
 
No.
 
55,
 
Series
 
of
 
2024.
 
The
 
ICO
 
is
 
mandated
 
to
 
serve
 
as
 
the
 
central
 
communications
 
authority
 
of
 
SLSU,
 
tasked
 
with
 
strategic
 
communication
 
planning,
 
public
 
image
 
promotion,
 
reputation
 
management,
 
and
 
ensuring
 
brand
 
consistency
 
across
 
all
 
platforms.
 
The
 
ICO
 
Head,
 
appointed
 
by
 
the
 
University
 
President,
 
reports
 
directly
 
to
 
the
 
Director
 
of
 
the
 
Presidential
 
Management
 
Coordinating
 
Office
 
(PMCO).
   II.   Vision   To  be  a  premier  communications  hub  that  fosters  transparency,  engagement,  and  excellence,  
shaping
 
a
 
positive
 
and
 
influential
 
public
 
image
 
for
 
Southern
 
Luzon
 
State
 
University.
   III.   Mission   The  Information  and  Communications  Office  (ICO)  is  dedicated  to:  1.  Developing  and  implementing  strategic  communication  plans  that  align  with  SLSU’s  
objectives;
 2.  Promoting  and  safeguarding  the  university’s  public  image  and  reputation;  3.  Ensuring  consistent  and  high-quality  branding  across  all  communication  channels;  4.  Providing  expert  communication  services  and  support  to  internal  and  external  
stakeholders;
 
and,
 5.  Enhancing  stakeholder  engagement  through  effective  information  dissemination  and  
creative
 
content
 
development.
   IV.   Service  Pledge   We,  the  members  of  the  Information  and  Communications  Office  (ICO),  pledge  to:  ●  Uphold  the  highest  standards  of  professionalism  and  integrity  in  all  our  communications.  ●  Deliver  accurate,  timely,  and  relevant  information  to  all  stakeholders.  ●  Foster  a  culture  of  transparency  and  accountability  within  the  university.  ●  Provide  exceptional  communication  services,  including  report  writing,  technical  writing,  
graphic
 
design,
 
event
 
coverage,
 
and
 
multimedia
 
production.
 ●  Continuously  improve  our  communication  strategies  and  practices  to  better  serve  the  
SLSU
 
community
 
and
 
its
 
stakeholders.
